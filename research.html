From Pixels to Insight: Enhancing Metallic Component Defect Detection with GLCM Features and AI Explainability
Varsha, A., Mundra, K., Singh, A., Bhosale, H., Chattopadhyay, C., Valadi, J.
Data Management, Analytics and Innovation — ICDMAI 2024. Lecture Notes in Networks and Systems, vol 997. Springer, Singapore.

Presents a machine learning pipeline for detecting surface defects in metallic components using GLCM (Gray-Level Co-occurrence Matrix) texture features. Achieved 89% classification accuracy on the NEU Surface Defect Dataset. Integrated SHAP-based explainability to make model decisions interpretable for industrial quality control applications.

AI Coding Assistants in Competitive Programming: Empirical Studies from India on Human-AI Interactions in Learning, Problem Solving, and Curiosity Development Perspectives
Joshi, O., Mundra, K., Yadav, D., Das, S.
Artificial Intelligence in Education — AIED 2025. Communications in Computer and Information Science, vol 2590. Springer, Cham.

An empirical study examining how AI coding assistants (Amazon Q, Tabnine) affect learning outcomes, problem-solving behaviour, and curiosity in competitive programming contexts. Based on structured evaluations across 20+ participants at IIT Bombay, with findings contributing to the broader understanding of human-AI collaboration in educational settings.
