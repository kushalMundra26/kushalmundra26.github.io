Marginal-Gain Multi-Agent Auction Bot
Warwick · 2025

Designed an intelligent auction bot grounded in Cobb-Douglas utility theory. The willingness-to-pay formula was derived from the product scoring function's partial derivatives. Incorporated bid shading, a competition bonus, and a two-component bid formula for strategic precision.

Built opponent detection for four adversary archetypes. Achieved a majority win rate in a 10-bot, 50-auction tournament with an average score exceeding 13,500.

Python Game Theory Opponent Modelling Cobb-Douglas Multi-Agent Systems
